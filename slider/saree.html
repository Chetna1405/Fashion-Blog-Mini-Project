Best Saree & Blouses Colour Combinations for Weddings

Modern Saree Colour Combinations That Look Sensational
Green and Maroon This bright and illuminating combination is ideal for weddings, grand functions, or puja ceremonies. It looks stunning and emanates good vibes and prosperity.

Red and Blue This vibrant yet sober combination pairs beautifully with rustic accessories. It serves as an excellent canvas for traditional zari or temple work, making it a must-have in your wardrobe.

Purple and Mustard A bold and unique combination, perfect for the wedding season. Brides often pair this striking combination with a golden blouse to shine throughout the day. Purple and mustard are also easy on the eyes.


Green and Pink A statement piece that works for any occasion. It can be a somber corporate attire for office meetings or an eye-catching outfit for day functions. This combination looks fabulous with heavy decorative embellishments.

Yellow and Pink A trending combination, yellow with subtle pink designs or borders is marvelous for all kinds of occasions. Pair it with a contrasting blouse to enhance your skin tone.
